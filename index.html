ikMobile
Connecting You-sers...

A smartphone simulator for PCs with support for voice call and text messaging (through a built-in or USB modem), and many more features.

No need to link your phone to emulate. No need to connect your phone with a cable. It doesn't require hardware acceleration and runs on virtually any PC.
